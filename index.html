About Us- Remo-Stars-FC

Remo Stars FC, the Sky Blue Stars,is a top Nigeria football
club based in ikenne, Ogun State. Founded in 2010 by
Kunle Soname, we have risen through the ranks, earning
a historic 3rd place in the 2021-22 NPFL season and a
CAF Confederation Cup spot.

Playing at the 5,000-capacity Remo Stars stadium, we are
committed to excellence, talent development, and community
impact. Led by Chairman Kunle Soname and Coach Daniel Ogunmodede,
we strive for greatness in Nigerian and African football.

About the Founder-- Kunle Soname

Kunle Soname, is a visonary entrpreneur and
football enthusiast from Ogun State,founded in 2004.
His mission was to build a professional football institution
that nurtures young Nigerian talents and provides them
with opportunities to compete at the highest levels.

Through his leadership, Remo Stars has grown into
a respectable club Known for excellence,integrity,and
youth development. His passion for football continue
to shape the future of Nigeriasports, placing Remo Stars
on the map as a rising force in Afican football.
